ABOUT ME

My journey into this work began in Family Law in Brazil, where I witnessed the complexity of family relationships and became increasingly drawn to what lies beneath visible patterns of conflict. This led me to train professionally in Family Constellations and systemic work.

Alongside my legal background, years of study in transgenerational dynamics and spirituality have shaped a broad, ethical, and compassionate approach to my practice.

I create and facilitate spaces for systemic exploration where people can reconnect with themselves, their relationships, and their family histories. These processes support greater awareness, respect, and openness, allowing life to flow more naturally as each person reconnects with their place within the system.
